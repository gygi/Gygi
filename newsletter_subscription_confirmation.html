Welcome to Orson Gygi!

Our story begins in 1945 in the small mountainous valley of Salt Lake City, Utah where Orson Gygi opened a brick and mortar storefront specializing in restaurant wholesale kitchen supplies. However, as the needs of his customers changed, so did the inventory on his shelves.
 
Today, Orson Gygi is a specialty kitchen store that provides everything from essentials, to the niche, quirky, and boutique. Our goal is to get to know our customers and provide the kitchen experience they're looking for. 

If you’re local, come in and say hi. If you’re not, let’s create, inspire, and engage with each other anyways. You can follow our blog for unique kitchen inspiration, shop for all your kitchen and party needs, or just wait for us to send you great ideas and promotions through our newsletter. 
 
We strive to keep our grandfather's (and great grandfather's) principle values as the Gygi brand is passed from our family to yours.
